Zombies

How To Play

Story

  • To check use the "]" key when you are playing
  • As the rounds increase the zombies will come of diffrent types
  • There will be 5 levels
  • The final level will have a boss
  • To get through the the round, you will need to kill all of the zombies
  • If you want to do endless, you will need to kill the ball at level 5 after you kill the boss, the buttton will be there for only 10 seconds to decide
  • The boss will attack by placing bombs anywhere on the map and after a few seconds, the bomb will explade, if the player is close to the bomb, the player will be knocked back and will lose some health

Endless

  • In the endless gamemode, you will be presented with a diffrent map
  • There are continous rounds with an increasing amount of zombies
  • As the rounds increase, the monsters will coume with an increase in health for every round
  • To get Past each round, you will need to kill all of the zombies
  • To shoot, aim using your mouse pointer and left click to shoot
  • In every round, there will be powerups that you can pickup, these powerups will last 20 seconds
    • White Monster Can: Freeze the monsters
    • Yellow Monster Can: Half Monster Health
    • Green Monster Can: Double Bullet Damage
    • Original Monster Can: Invinsibility
  • There are obsticles around the map that the player or monsters can't pass through but arrows and bullets can pass through
  • After you kil the boss, you has 10 seconds to decide if you want to continute continue the game
  • Every round after you continue, the monster helth increases so, will get harder.
  • You can also upgraade the players stats (player health and the bullets damage) which is bought by coins which you get by killing monsters
  • To shoot, you will first need to pick up a gun by walking over it, you will then have 60 bullets with 6 bullets in the chamber, once all of the 6 bullets are used, you will need to reload by click the r key on your keyboard
